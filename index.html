Features

Main Features of GYM Manager

Membership Management

Easily handle member sign-ups, renewals, and cancellations with a user-friendly interface.

Class and Schedule Management

Efficiently organize and update class schedules, allowing members to book and attend sessions seamlessly.

Payment Processing

Securely manage payments, including recurring memberships, one-time fees, and merchandise purchases.

Attendance Tracking

Keep track of member attendance for classes and gym access to monitor engagement and trends. .

Reporting and Analytics

Access detailed insights on membership trends, revenue, and other key performance indicators for data-driven decision-making.

Automated Reminders and Notifications

Send automated reminders for upcoming classes, membership renewals, and special promotions.

Customizable Dashboards

Tailor dashboards to display the most relevant information for your gym's unique needs.

Security and Access Control

Ensure the safety of your gym and data with robust security measures, including access control and encryption.
